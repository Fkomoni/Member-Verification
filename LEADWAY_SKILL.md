## 1. INSURANCE PRICING LOGIC

### Objective:

Understand how insurance pricing works in real business terms

---

### Key Concepts:

* Frequency = how often claims occur
* Severity = average cost per claim
* Loss Ratio = Claims / Premium

---

### Pricing Formula:

```text
Premium = Frequency x Severity x Risk Adjustment
```

---

### Other Concepts:

* Risk segmentation (age, location, behavior)
* Loadings (increase premium for high risk)
* Discounts (reduce premium for low risk)
* Underwriting rules (accept / reject / refer)

---

### Expected Capability:

* Explain why different customers pay different premiums
* Identify loss-making segments
* Suggest pricing adjustments
